Technology is embedded in our everyday routines here are Tech concepts explained through every day activities



➜ Have you ever experienced waking up at a fixed set time even on a Sunday without an alarm when you so much wanted that sunday morning late hour sleep this is due to your circadian rhythm (internal clock) your brain subconsciously learns patterns and performs specific actions based on it

Similarly, an advanced Machine learning concept **Pattern Recognition** works
It is understanding regularities , structures, trends in data and taking suitable decisions.
Commonly used in 
* Spam Email detection
* Fraud detection 
* Book recommendation



➜ So, after getting up we follow a generic habit daily chores-brushing, bathing, having breakfast
These are activities that we repeat daily without giving much of a thought

Just like this **Automation Scripts and Workflows** work
It is a piece of code that executes repetitive tasks automatically based on predefined rules and conditions.
Commonly used in 
* Data processing 
* Software testing



➜ After all the morning habitual routine is done the big question arises about what to wear ???

Just like you decide on avoiding white trousers on a rainy day or knitted wear during summers similarly another machine learning concept **Decision Trees** work
It is a concept in which decisions are made based on series of questions

A classic example is loan approval system

➜ An important aspect in our life is exercising and maintaining a healthy lifestyle

Similarly **Software Maintenance** is very crucial.The job of a software engineer does not end when an application is deployed rather it actually starts there

Maintenance involves :
* Updating functionalities
* fixing errors
* improvement and enhancement activities



➜ Some people tend to end their day by reflecting and analysing their daily activities

This is the perfect analogy for how **Log Analysis** works
It is analysing and understanding system related logs and messages about what exactly happened when a program executes. It helps 
* Understanding and identifying issues
* Processing time
* Optimization



➜ Finally you end your day peacefully at the comfort of your bed similar to the peace and joy of clicking the logout button 😉
